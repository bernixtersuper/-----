Manga

Here are some websites i have used to read manga in japanese online as well as download it and read it offline. If you want to read manga in a mobile device i highly recommend checking out:

Tachiyomi

Its an android app that allows you to read manga from different sources, you can download the manga and read it offline, i recommend using the local source option. Theres a guide on Tachiyomi's website on how to set up different types of files but basically you are gonna need to hava a folder named Tachiyomi and in it another one called local, here you can store folders with the chapters/volumes of the manga you downloaded from nyaa.si or mangazip and keep track of your progress.

Downloading Manga

When it comes to downloading manga Nyaa.si is the best website i have found, it has a huge selection of not only manga but also anime and books. You can download the manga and read it offline with a manga reader like Tachiyomi or OpenComic. To download the contents you are gonna need a torrent client like qBittorrent, otherwise you can use another source like mangazip that lets you download manga through downloaders like sakurafile or rapidgator

Online readers

For online casual reading any online reading website listed below will do the job just fine, Just make sure to have an adblock installed on your browser for the best experience.

Manga GUN

Lots of raw manga ready to read, its the best for online casual reading

Rawkuma

Online reader that allows individual chapter downloads good to download recent chapters of ongoing manga

Nyaa.si

Best for downloading manga and reading it offline, huge selection of not only manga but also anime and books

Tachiyomi

Android app that allows you to read manga from different sources, you can download the manga and read it offline

OpenComic

Highly customizable windows reader, download the setup.exe file from the repository

qBittorrent

My go-to open-source Torrent client
