趣味と知恵

"Shumi to chie" (hobby and knowledge) is a website dedicated to document the useful tools i have encountered while pursuing my hobbies on the internet. Since i wish i had known them sooner i will showcase them for you in this site. In each section i will provide a description of the tools i use the most and how to use them. I hope you find them useful and that they help you in your journey to learn new things.

On my japanese learning journey i have found that the best way to learn is to have fun while doing it, so i have included some of the tools i use to read manga and watch anime in japanese. Enjoy!
